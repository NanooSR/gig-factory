# Lead Value ROI Calculator

## Target buyer
Freelancers, coaches, and small local businesses running paid acquisition:
- lead-gen agencies
- social media managers
- creators running subscription or local service funnels
- small B2B consultancies with paid campaigns

## Problem this solves
Many service providers bid on leads but don’t know what conversion rate makes a campaign profitable. This gig provides a fast decision tool to forecast new-client volume, gross profit, and return on ad spend from two key conversion stages.

## What is included
- Inputs for lead flow, conversion rates, average ticket, margin, and ad spend
- ROI math with break-even client threshold
- Cost-per-lead and margin-based interpretation
- Real-time brand customization (name/email/CTA)
- Email CTA prefilled with a campaign snapshot

## Customization ideas
- Add a third row for recurring revenue vs one-time revenue
- Add custom conversion stages for consultative funnels
- Include recommended budget bands (safe / aggressive) as colored ranges
- Localize currency or language for international markets
- Add screenshot of client branding and testimonials to increase social proof
