Campaign assumptions

How this scenario is calculated

Projected qualified leads = monthly leads × qualification rate. Projected new clients = qualified leads × client conversion rate. Estimated gross profit = projected clients × revenue per client × (1 + additional customer value) × gross margin. Gross-profit ROI = (estimated gross profit − ad spend) ÷ ad spend. Results are estimates based only on the assumptions entered.
